Classic Beef Lasagna

Lasagna is one of those dishes that rewards patience but doesn’t need complexity. At its core, it’s just good meat sauce, a proper béchamel, and careful layering — nothing fancy, nothing rushed. Done right, it’s comforting, rich, and even better the next day. This is a classic, reliable version you can make anytime and trust completely.

    Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500 g (1 lb) minced beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 800 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g flour
  • 600 ml milk (warm)
  • Salt, pepper
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Sauté onion until soft (5–7 min).
  3. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds.
  4. Add beef. Cook until browned.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, basil.
  6. Stir in tomato paste.
  7. Add tomatoes (+ wine if using).
  8. Simmer gently 20–30 minutes until thick.
  9. Make the béchamel
  10. Assemble
  11. Bake
