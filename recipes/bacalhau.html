Bacalhau à Zé do Pipo

Description

A classic Portuguese comfort dish: tender cod, creamy mashed potatoes, sweet onions, olive oil, and a lightly baked mayonnaise topping.

    Ingredients:
  • 600 g salted cod (bacalhau), soaked and desalted
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 small onion (for boiling)
  • 1 kg potatoes, peeled
  • 50 ml milk (approx.)
  • 30 g butter
  • Salt & white pepper, to taste
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 150 ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 200–250 g mayonnaise (homemade or good-quality store-bought)
  • Optional: black olives for garnish
